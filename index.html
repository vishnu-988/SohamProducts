About Us

Soham Products

At Soham Products, we specialize in manufacturing premium-quality brass components, proudly serving industries with precision and excellence since 1989. Based in Jamnagar, the "Brass City of India," we bring over 35 years of experience in delivering exceptional brass products that meet the highest standards of quality and reliability.

Our state-of-the-art facilities and skilled team ensure that every product is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, tailored to meet the unique requirements of our clients. Whether you need Brass fittings, custom components, or castings, Soham Products is your trusted partner for reliable and high-quality brass solutions.

We are committed to balancing top-tier quality, timely delivery, and cost-effective solutions to exceed customer expectations. At Soham Products, we take pride in being a dependable partner, empowering industries with brass components that set benchmarks for performance and durability.
